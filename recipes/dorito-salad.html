Dorito Salad

Description

This Dorito Taco Salad is the perfect quick and easy, veggie-packed dinner! It's made with a fresh, crisp romaine, topped with ground beef, black beans, tomatoes, corn, and shredded cheese, then finished off with nacho Doritos and home-made salsa!

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Black Beans
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Diced Tomatoes
  • Romain Lettuce
  • Corn
  • Cheese
  • Homemade Salsa
  • Doritos

Steps

  1. Cook the beef: In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the beef until browned. Drain the grease or use paper towels to mop up the greae right in the pan.
  2. Add in the beans and taco seasoning. Cook and stir until combined
  3. Prep the veggies: Cut the tomato and squeeze out excess liquid, then combine lettuce, tomato, corn, and cheese in a large bowl.
  4. Combine and serve: Top the veggie mixture with the beef/beans, then stir in dressing. Right before you're ready to serve, top with Doritos!
