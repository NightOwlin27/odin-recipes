New Year's Eve Sherbet Punch

Description

This sherbet punch is such a fun, and sweet bubbly drink. It makes a lot, is perfect for any get togethers, and especially suitable for a Christmas or New Year's Eve party!

Ingredients

  • Strawberry Kool-Aid powder
  • Water
  • Pineapple Juice
  • Cranberry Ginger Ale -- or regular Ginger Ale
  • Pineapple Sherbet
  • Pineapple Rings
  • Raspberries

Steps

  1. Add the Strawberry Kool-Aid powder to a large punch bowl.
  2. Pour in the water and stir it together until the powder is totally dissolved
  3. Stir in the pineapple juice
  4. Stir in the ginger ale
  5. Add scoops of the sherbert into the punch bowl
  6. Add pineapple rings and raspberries, and serve immediately
